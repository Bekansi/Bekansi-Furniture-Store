About Bekansi

Ethiopia's Trusted Name in Furniture & Interior Design

For over a decade, Bekansi Furniture & Interior Design has been transforming houses, apartments, offices and hotels across Addis Ababa into elegant, comfortable living spaces. We combine modern design sensibility with authentic Ethiopian craftsmanship to build furniture that lasts.

From custom-made sofas and bedroom sets to full kitchen cabinet installations and complete interior design packages, our skilled carpenters, upholsterers and designers work together to deliver quality you can feel and elegance you can see.

  • Locally manufactured, premium materials
  • Custom sizing & personalized designs
  • Free in-home design consultation
  • Delivery & installation across Ethiopia
