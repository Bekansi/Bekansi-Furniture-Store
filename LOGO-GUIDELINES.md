---

## 📏 Logo Usage

### Minimum Clear Space
Keep at least **16 pixels** of clear space around the logo on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- **Desktop:** 170×42 px (recommended)
- **Mobile:** 140×35 px (minimum)
- **Favicon:** 32×32 px and up

### Don't...
❌ Stretch or distort the logo
❌ Rotate the logo
❌ Change the colors
❌ Add shadows or effects without approval
❌ Use the logo on backgrounds that reduce contrast

## 🔄 Converting to Other Formats

To use the logo in other formats:

1. **PNG Version:**
   - Export SVG as PNG at 2x resolution (e.g., 340×84 for logo)
   - Use online tool: https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png

2. **PDF Version:**
   - Export SVG as PDF for print materials
   - Use: https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-pdf

3. **PNG Favicon:**
   - Use: https://icoconvert.com/ to convert SVG to .ico
   - Or export as 32×32 PNG
